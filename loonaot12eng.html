LOONA (이달의 소녀)
Members:
Haseul, Heejin, Haseul, YeoJin, ViVi, Kim Lip, JinSoul, Choerry, Yves, Chuu, Gowon, Olivia Hye

LOONA (Hangul: 이달 의 소녀, Idalui Sonyeo), styled as LOOΠΔ, is a South Korean girl group under BlockBerryCreative. The group consists of 12 members: HeeJin, HyunJin, HaSeul, YeoJin, ViVi, Kim Lip, JinSoul, Choerry, Yves, Chuu, Go Won and Olivia Hye; and three subunits: LOOΠΔ 1/3, ODD EYE CIRCLE and yyxy. From October 2016 to June 2018, each member of the group was revealed one by one, along with a single album and music video for the title track. The full group debuted on August 19, 2018 on their show LOONAbirth with the release of [++] on August 20, 2018. During the MV scenes, the other three girls are watching ViVi, like in the scene where she shoots the basketball in basket easily and quickly. The scene that most broke my heart in this MV was when ViVi was at break watching the other students eating and talking, while she was alone. Then she opens her school locker and we can see several juice boxes that the school distributes to students, all full. It's obvious that robots don't eat or drink liquids, so I got this tightness in my heart because she seems to have feelings, and she just wants to be a normal girl. At the end of the MV, ViVi runs out of school without carrying her backpack (which she unplugs) and ends up running out of battery, stopping in the middle of the street.

Sobre BlockBerry

Blockberry Creative é uma gravadora sul-coreana formada em 2015. Atualmente fazendo parte do grupo Ilkwang. A empresa é a casa do atual grupo feminino LOONA que debutou em agosto de 2018.
